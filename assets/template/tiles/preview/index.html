The Great Gatsby


Synopsis - The Great Gatsby is a romantic novel written by american author F. Scott Fitzgerald. The story of a man who believes his lost romantic relationship can be regained by exhibiting his newly created wealth and social status. Gatsby an army officer, falls in love with Daisy. During his serving at world war, Daisy marries a rich but brutal man Tom Buchanan. Gatsby returns after world war and went on to create huge wealth through unethical practices.
